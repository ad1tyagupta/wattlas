Project state is not evidence quality

announcedplanning filedpermittedunder constructionoperationalpausedcancelled

A low-confidence “permitted” claim remains permitted-but-low-confidence; it is never silently relabelled. Expansions live as separate project phases with their own capacity, dates, and state history.

Hard rules for honest uncertainty

  • Missing is null, never zero. Estimated values are visibly marked and widen the range.
  • Conflicts are retained. Material disagreement lowers confidence and appears in the dossier.
  • Freshness is metric-specific. Operational feeds can stale in days; planning and climate baselines in months.
  • Insufficient coverage produces “not yet rankable,” not a deceptively precise number.
  • Lifecycle scenarios stay visible: committed, probable, and announced-upside—rather than one hidden realization assumption.
